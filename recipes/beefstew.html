Beef Stew

Full of flavor, two pounds of tender beef, and loaded with vegetables, this recipe is sure to be a hit for anyone who enjoys a good hearty meal. It is especially great for winter occasions.

Ingredients

Makes 8-10 servings

  • 2 Pounds Stew Beef, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 5 Teaspoons Oil
  • 1 Cup Red Wine or Cooking Wine
  • 4 Cups Beef Broth
  • 2 Medium Onions
  • 3 Medium Yellow Potatoes
  • 4 Carrots
  • 1/3 Cup FLour
  • 2 Tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Ground Pepper

Preparation

  1. Combine the flour, garlic powder, and ground pepper in a freezer bag. Add the beef and shake the bag to coat the beef.
  2. Heat the oil in a large cast iron pot.
  3. Add the beef to the pot, browning on all sides. You will need to do this in batches. Add oil as needed.
  4. Remove the beef and add the wine to the pot to deglaze. Scrape with a wooden spoon until the browned bits are scraped off.
  5. Add the beef back to the pot with the beef broth. Bring to a boil, then simmer and cover for 2 hours.
  6. Add the sliced potatoes and carrots. Simmer for 45 minutes. Add additional broth or water as needed.
  7. Season with salt to taste. It is best served with toasted bread or pasta.
